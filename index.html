Trip Details

Destination
Cebu, Philippines
Dates
July 2 - July 17, 2026
Cost
$4,000 (includes airfare, lodging, meals, and project materials)
Ages
Youth builders between ages 16 and 19

What's Included

  • Round-trip international airfare
  • Lodging and meals
  • Project materials and worksite coordination
  • Two Trip Leaders and Two Parent Builders
  • Sightseeing experiences unique to Cebu

Project Details

HXP Builders will help build and renovate classroom spaces for a local school facing overcrowded buildings. You'll work alongside community members to create a safe, lasting learning environment for young students.

Itinerary

  1. Days 1-3: Arrival, Orientation, & Excursions
    Travel to Cebu, meet your fellow builders, and explore the city.
  2. Days 4-9: Building Projects
    Serve on the worksite with local community members to build classrooms and playgrounds.
  3. Days 10-11: 2nd Excursion and Church Activities with Local Branch
    Sightseeing and participation in local church services.
  4. Days 12-16: Building Projects
    Continue building projects and wrap up the experience.
  5. Days 17-18: Reflection and Returning Home
    Reflect on the experience and travel back home.
